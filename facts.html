Facts about Taylor Swift

fact #1

Taylor Swift is named after James Taylor, one of her parents’ favorite artists.

fact #2

Taylor Swift grew up on a Christmas tree farm (which inspired her song “Christmas Tree Farm”). Her job was to pick praying mantis pods off the trees before they were sold.

fact #3

The day before her Reputation tour began in 2018, Swift invited 2,000 adopted and foster children to a private dress rehearsal.

fact #4

Taylor wrote a 350-page novel when she was 12, which, so far, has never been published.

fact #5

Taylor’s first album was released in October 2006 and sold more than 2.5 million copies!
